BZRAs Currently Prescribed

BZRA Regimen Details

This page is designed to capture the starting point for the medication tapering. If the patient is tapering their current medication, enter the dose that is currently being taken. This may be different from the dose that was prescribed. You may want to consider switching your patient to a new medication before tapering. If this is the case, choose the new medication from the drop down list and enter the starting dose for this new medication.

Note

Some BZRAs are not easily tapered due to dosage formulation limitation. This includes capsules (chlordiazepoxide, clorazepate, flurazepam, temazepam) and specially formulated tablets (zolpidem orally disintegrating tablets). You may wish to switch to an equivalent dose of an alternative BZRA available in multiple tablet strengths that can further split into smaller doses. Taper schedule are available for shorter (oxazepam), intermediate (lorazepam), and longer (clonazepam) half-life BZRAs.
